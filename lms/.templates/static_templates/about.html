Who We Are.

We deliver quality education to everyone, everywhere

Impact Story & Timeline

In 2020, when Corona Pandemic hit the nation, many children stayed out of school and education for an elongated period of time. BloomED came in to close access gaps and provide quality engagement with learning even when away from teachers or the school.

We have moved many steps further now. BloomED is a personalized tutor for all learning needs. All our resources are available for free except when needing to get support from a teacher.

OUR MISSION

Our actions, policies and dedication are squarely dictated by our mission of:

  • Provide universal access to affordable and high-quality education for everyone.
  • Make learning entirely personalized.
  • Blend classroom and online learning.
